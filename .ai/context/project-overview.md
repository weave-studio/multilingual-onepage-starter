## About This Template

### What is This?

The **Multilingual One-Page Starter** is a professional, production-proven Eleventy template designed for building fast, accessible, multilingual one-page websites. It was extracted and generalized from the [Weave Studio](https://weavewebdesign.com) production website.

### Target Audience

This template is ideal for:

- **Agencies** building client sites that need multilingual support
- **Freelancers** who want a solid foundation with modern best practices
- **Businesses** creating international landing pages or portfolios
- **Developers** who value accessibility, performance, and maintainability
- **Projects** requiring RTL language support (Hebrew, Arabic, Farsi, etc.)
